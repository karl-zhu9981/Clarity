The Thermostat

Clarity also submitted a prototype for the physical thermostat. Some information about it:

1. The three buttons are used to change the temperature. The top and bottoms button raise and lower the temperature respectively. The middle button is required in order to 'set' any temperature change.

2. The thermostat comes with LED lights on the bottom that indicate your expenditure on heating/cooling. A green light means better than average: anything below $1900/year. A yellow light means average: $1900-$2500. A red light means you're spending more than average: above $2500.
